# Narrative QA templates

AP-134 requires these records before a chapter is released. Templates contain no participant data or results. Store consented research records in the approved research system, not this repository.

## Writer read-through

- Content version / chapter / reviewer / date:
- Route(s) read, including fail-forward and zero-capture route:
- Does every transition follow from established player knowledge? Record node IDs:
- Does the recap match the route actually taken? Record node IDs:
- Does each ending avoid collection-rate, rarity, night-travel, and precise-location requirements?
- Continuity defects, severity, owner, and retest result:

## Target-user comprehension and emotional-response study

Recruit **8–12 target users** with informed consent. Do not collect names, precise location, contact details, recordings, or raw free text in source control.

Measure after the vertical slice:

1. Can the participant identify the player role?
2. Can they state the central conflict?
3. Can they explain why they would continue exploring?
4. Did they understand the consequence of their most recent choice?
5. Was any content distressing, coercive, unsafe, or unclear?

Record only aggregated outcomes in the approved research system. The production threshold is defined by the product owner before the study; a static lint result is not user-test evidence.
